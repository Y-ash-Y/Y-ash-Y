### About

Final-year **B.S. Physics** student at **IIT Kanpur** (minors in Economics & Machine Learning). My work shares one spine: **principled control of false alarms** — Neyman–Pearson and false-discovery-rate calibration — and I keep proving the same discipline transfers across unrelated domains.

I care about honest evaluation. Most of my projects report what *didn't* work and why, not just the headline metric — because a detector you can trust is one that's calibrated about its own mistakes.

### Featured work

Each project is tagged with the false-alarm control method it relies on.

**[LABAD — Insider-Threat UEBA & Behavioral Anomaly Detection](https://github.com/Y-ash-Y/LABAD)**
*Neyman–Pearson @ 1% FPR.* A BiLSTM autoencoder on CERT r4.2 plus a from-scratch Bayesian Online Changepoint Detector that localizes *when* a compromise began, with RAG threat reports over 714 MITRE ATT&CK techniques via an air-gapped LLM.
`AUC 0.87` · `100% Precision@10` · `onset to a median of 3 days`

**[PMSA — Calibrated, Explainable AI-Image Detector](https://github.com/Y-ash-Y/AI-Detected-)**
*Split-conformal Neyman–Pearson.* A frozen SigLIP backbone fused with a hand-crafted forensic stream, with a finite-sample guarantee on the false-alarm rate. Honest in-the-wild evaluation and few-shot adaptation to brand-new generators.
`0.90 AUC (Chameleon)` · `83–91% detection @ 1% FPR`

**[Statistical Arbitrage — Causal, FDR-Controlled Pairs Trading](https://github.com/Y-ash-Y/StatArb)**
*Benjamini–Hochberg FDR.* Took a backtest reporting Sharpe 4.40, proved it was fiction, diagnosed why (universal decointegration), and located the real binding constraint — execution cost.
`242 pairs screened` · `~5 bps/leg break-even`

**[VoteChain — Decentralized Voting on Ethereum](https://github.com/Y-ash-Y/DecentralizedVotingSystem)**
*On-chain commit–reveal.* A full-stack Solidity dApp with role-based access control and a genuine on-chain privacy scheme, live on Sepolia. Includes a real repository incident-response on a leaked deployer key.
`48-case Hardhat suite` · `live on Sepolia`

**[BB84 QKD — Sub-Threshold Eavesdropping Detection](https://github.com/Y-ash-Y/Quantum_research)** · *Research*
*Decoy-ratio coverage tests.* A theory-validated simulator showing the standard QBER check is blind to attacks engineered below it — and that cheap ML on the right channel statistics is not.
`87% vs 0.8% detection` · `PNS: AUC 1.00 vs 0.51`

**[Lunar Elemental Mapping (ISRO)](https://github.com/Y-ash-Y/ISRO_LUNAR_MISSION)** · *Inter-IIT Tech Meet 13.0*
An end-to-end XRF pipeline over five years of Chandrayaan-2 CLASS data, producing kilometer-scale elemental maps with per-pixel uncertainty. Placed **6th of 23 IITs**.
